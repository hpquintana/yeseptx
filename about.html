About

First, let us tell you what we're not.

#yesEPTX is not another accelerator, agency, angel group, app, authority, bank, blog, co-working space, commercializer, contest, council, firm, fund, incubator, infrastructure, institution, movement, non-profit, platform, program, song, or university. There are already enough of those in town (arguably).

We are a growing community committed to improving El paso's startup ecosystem by helping each other.

In other words, you are #yesEPTX. Your agenda is our agenda. It’s time you had an easier way to get loud about your successes, hurdles, failures, needs, and vision. Just as importantly, it’s time you hear what other local players are doing, experiencing, feeling, needing, and wanting in the valley.

No more lack of visibility into what’s happening around town. No more passing like ships in the night. No more focusing on just ourselves. Together, we will send a clear message to the startup ecosystem both in and beyond Texas that, yes, El Paso is a viable marketplace for ventures to launch, grow, and succeed, on it's way to becoming a vibrant one. Or in blunter terms:

Stuff's happening.

The shift has already started. Maybe you’ve heard the underground grumblings or maybe you haven’t. Maybe you’ve witnessed the ecosystem’s changes and increasing opportunity or maybe you’re still dreaming about it. Maybe you’ve found great success accomplishing your goals with the local resources accessible to you or maybe you’ve had trouble. Whatever your experience, position, or perspective, there’s more to be done.

Get involved. We will do this. Together.
