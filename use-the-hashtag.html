Use the Hashtag

Be creative, but consistent.

That’s the simple version, at least. The longer version is this: use the #yesEPTX hashtag everywhere you think it could apply, so that we collectively amplify what’s happening in Texas’ startup ecosystem.

Here are some ideas as you begin to use the #yesEPTX hashtag...

Write about what you or your startup are doing.

Whether you’re launching a new business, a new product, or just new feature, be sure to let the El Paso community know.

Broadcast great tips, advice, quotes, and ideas.

Even if the content isn’t originating in El Paso, Texas; there are lots of great resources out there that we love accessing as a community. So, share the wealth.

Be honest about your successes and struggles.

Don’t be shy. Startup life is hard. Be honest about where you’re encountering trouble, especially as it relates to the El Paso startup community.

Share your vision for El Paso TX.

If there’s something you want to see in El Paso, be the voice of change and tell the community what you want to see. We’re counting on you to help us communicate the vision for vibrancy.

Your voice matters. Get involved
