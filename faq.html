FAQs

Let's answer those pressing questions.

Who started #yesEPTX?

Who started #yesEPTX? The small group of guys who started #yesEPTX like to think that they gave a name to what was already happening in the valley more so than they started something new. But if you must know, #yesEPTX was instigated by Humberto Valle.

What is the goal of #yesEPTX?

In a word: vibrancy. While we believe El Paso is a viable marketplace for venture activity, it is not yet a vibrant one. Learn about our vision for Texas’ startup ecosystem.

How does #yesEPTX benefit me as a startup founder/investor/etc.?

Wherever you’re at in the lifecycle of your career or startup, there’s little denying that El Paso has a long way to go before becoming a vibrant marketplace for venture activity. Read our vision to learn how you individually helping others now will collectively result in the long-term improvement of Texas’ startup ecosystem.

What is the best incubator or accelerator for startups in town?

This is a question we hope to answer collectively based upon candid feedback from program participants and their resulting successes. Until we are able to gather the facts and create such a report, we would encourage you reach out directly to prior participants in local incubators and accelerators for their own opinions. A few of the most notable incubators and accelerators in town include AREA 48, CEIA, Desarrollo,High Tide, Mac6", and SeedSpot.

Is #yesEPTX a non-profit or for-profit organization?

Neither. We like to think of ourselves as an organized we-all-profit — more evolving organism than enterprising organization. We will rely entirely upon the community’s support of one another to see our vision for vibrancy become a reality. Put plainly, we cannot do this without you.

Can I give money to #yesEPTX?

Rather than waste your money on the operational costs an organization would require, we encourage that you instead:

  • Engage community members (especially true believers) at their fair market value for services you or your venture need; and
  • Offer your services pro bono to help us accomplish a variety of initiatives.

How can I help #yesEPTX?

There are a few ways to get involved with #yesEPTX, but even our list of ideas isn’t exhaustive. Be creative. If you have other ways you would like to help the cause, just go for it and let us know what you end up doing.

Who do I ask to begin helping #yesEPTX?

Don’t ask permission, just get involved. #yesEPTX is owned by all of us, you included.

I want to learn more. Who should I talk with about #yesEPTX?

#yesEPTX is an evolving organism that has taken on its own life. You can talk with any of our true believers to gain their perspectives. Or, reach out to any one of our instigators.

More answers will come as you get involved.
